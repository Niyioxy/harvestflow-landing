FAQ

Questions we get asked

Everything you need to know before registering your church.

Is my church's data secure?
+
Each church has a fully isolated workspace. No church can see another's data. All data is encrypted and stored securely on enterprise-grade infrastructure.
What happens after beta?
+
Early adopters will always be rewarded. When paid plans launch, beta churches get grandfathered pricing — significantly lower than new sign-ups. You'll never pay more for joining first.
Do workers need smartphones?
+
Any modern phone with a browser works. No app download required. Workers just need a phone number and browser access — even basic Android phones are supported.
Does it work without internet?
+
Yes. Workers can view their converts and log calls offline. Everything syncs automatically when back online. Perfect for remote outreach areas with limited connectivity.
How long does setup take?
+
Less than 24 hours. Register today, we set up your workspace, you get your login and can start adding converts immediately. Our team handles everything — no technical knowledge required.
Can I import existing convert lists?
+
Yes. HarvestFlow supports bulk CSV import. Upload your existing spreadsheet and all your converts are in the system instantly. We also provide a template to format your data correctly.
